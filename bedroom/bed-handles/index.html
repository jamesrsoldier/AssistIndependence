Bed Handle Buying Guide

Types of Bed Handles

Under-mattress handles slide between the mattress and foundation. Frame-mounted handles clamp to the bed frame. Floor-standing handles rest on the floor beside the bed. Choose based on your bed type and how much support you need.

Height and Reach

The handle should be at a comfortable height for gripping when lying down and when sitting on the edge of the bed. Most handles are 17-23 inches tall. Adjustable models let you customize the height for your needs.

Grip Design

Look for cushioned or ergonomic grips that are easy to hold, especially if you have arthritis or reduced grip strength. Some handles have multiple grip positions for different movements like sitting up versus standing up.

Additional Features

Storage pockets keep essentials like phones, glasses, and remotes within reach. Some handles include LED nightlights for safe navigation. Swing-away or folding designs make bed-making easier.

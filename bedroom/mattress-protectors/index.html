Mattress Protector Buying Guide

Types of Mattress Protectors

Fitted sheet style protectors wrap around the entire mattress with elastic edges. Encasement protectors fully enclose the mattress with a zipper for complete protection. Pad-style protectors lay on top and tuck under for easy removal and washing.

Waterproofing Technology

Modern protectors use polyurethane membranes that block liquids while allowing air to pass through. Avoid vinyl or plastic-backed protectors which trap heat and make noise. Look for products labeled as breathable waterproof for the best comfort.

Absorbency Levels

Light absorbency handles perspiration and minor spills. Moderate absorbency works for occasional incontinence. Heavy absorbency protectors have multi-layer cores designed for significant incontinence and can hold several cups of liquid.

Care and Durability

Check washing instructions before purchasing. Most quality protectors can be machine washed on gentle cycle and tumble dried on low. Avoid high heat which damages the waterproof layer. Look for protectors rated for 200+ wash cycles for long-term value.

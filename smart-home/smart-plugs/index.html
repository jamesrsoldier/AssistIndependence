Smart Plug Buying Guide

Best Uses for Seniors

Smart plugs are perfect for controlling lamps from bed or chair, scheduling coffee makers to start in the morning, turning off curling irons or space heaters automatically, and controlling hard-to-reach outlets. They give seniors independence by eliminating the need to bend or reach for switches.

Choosing the Right Size

Standard smart plugs may block the second outlet on a wall plate. Mini or compact smart plugs are designed to leave the second outlet accessible. If you plan to use multiple plugs on the same wall plate, choose compact models.

Voice Assistant Compatibility

Most smart plugs work with both Alexa and Google Assistant. Amazon's own smart plug only works with Alexa. If your parent uses a Google Nest speaker, make sure the plug supports Google Assistant. Check compatibility before purchasing.

Schedules and Routines

Smart plugs can turn devices on and off on a schedule (e.g., lamp on at 6 PM, off at 11 PM) or as part of routines (e.g., "Alexa, goodnight" turns off all plugs). Sunrise/sunset scheduling adjusts automatically throughout the year - great for outdoor lights.

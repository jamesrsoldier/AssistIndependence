About AssistIndependence

We believe home is where everyone deserves to be — safely, comfortably, and on their own terms.

Our Mission

At AssistIndependence, our mission is simple: help older adults and their families find the best aging-in-place products so they can continue living at home with confidence. We know that navigating the world of home safety products can feel overwhelming. There are thousands of options, conflicting reviews, and no shortage of sites that feel more like sales pitches than genuine help.

That is exactly why we exist. We are not a medical provider, and we are not here to push products on you. We are a trusted, warm guide — here to help you sort through the noise so you can make decisions that feel right for your home and your family.

How We're Different

Most product review sites send a team to test a handful of items and call it a day. We take a fundamentally different approach. Instead of relying on one person's opinion, we analyze thousands of verified reviews from Amazon and other major retailers to surface what real people trust most. We call this our real-reviews approach.

By looking at what hundreds — sometimes thousands — of real buyers consistently praise (and consistently warn about), we can identify patterns that a single reviewer never could. The result? Recommendations grounded in lived experience, not a single test in a lab.

We have no sponsored picks. No manufacturer pays to be featured. Our site is supported through affiliate partnerships, which means if you purchase a product through one of our links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This keeps our recommendations honest and our content free.

Who We Serve

We write for two groups of people who share the same goal: making home a place where independence thrives.

Adult caregivers (ages 45–65) — You may be researching products for a parent, in-law, or loved one. You want practical, trustworthy guidance without feeling like you are being sold to. You are juggling a lot, and you deserve a resource that respects your time and your intelligence. We are here to make your research easier and your decisions clearer.

Older adults (ages 65–80) — You value your independence and want to stay in the home you love for as long as possible. You are looking for products that genuinely help without making you feel like you have lost control. We write for you with the warmth and respect you deserve, because your comfort and dignity matter deeply to us.

Our Values

Independence

Every product we recommend is chosen because it empowers people to do more on their own, not less. The right grab bar, the right walker, the right kitchen tool — these are not signs of giving up. They are tools for living fully.

Dignity

Language matters. You will never see patronizing or clinical language on our site. We write with warmth and respect because the people we serve deserve nothing less. Everyone who visits this site is a capable adult making smart decisions for themselves or someone they love.

Trust

We recommend what we would want for our own families. Period. Every product on this site earned its place through the collective voice of real buyers, not because a company paid us to feature it. If we would not trust it for our own parents, it does not make the cut.

Honesty

We are transparent about how we operate. Our site is supported by affiliate commissions, which means we earn a small percentage when you buy through our links. This never influences which products we recommend or how we rank them. Our editorial decisions are made independently, and we disclose our affiliate relationships clearly on every page.

Our Commitment

We are committed to making this site accessible, useful, and honest. That means building pages that work well for people of all abilities, writing content that is clear and easy to read, and always putting the reader first. If something on our site is not working for you, we want to know about it.

We do not have all the answers, and we are not a substitute for professional medical or occupational therapy advice. But we are proud to be a starting point — a place where you can feel confident that the information you are reading is grounded in real experience and presented with genuine care.

Have questions? We'd love to hear from you.

Whether you have a suggestion, need help finding a product, or just want to say hello, our door is always open.

Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency is important to us. Here's how we keep AssistIndependence free and independent.

How AssistIndependence Is Supported

AssistIndependence is reader-supported. When you buy through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission at no additional cost to you. This is how we keep the site running and our recommendations free.

We believe in being upfront about how we make money so that you can trust the information and recommendations we provide. Affiliate commissions help us cover the costs of researching products, analyzing reviews, maintaining the website, and creating the free guides and resources you rely on.

Does This Affect Our Recommendations?

No. Our product selections are based entirely on real customer reviews and ratings — never on affiliate commission rates or sponsorships. We recommend what we would want for our own families, period.

We do not accept paid placements, sponsored product reviews, or any form of payment in exchange for a recommendation. Every product featured on our site earned its place through real customer feedback.

How Affiliate Links Work

When you click a product link on our site and make a purchase on the retailer's website (like Amazon), we may receive a small commission from that sale. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer's site — there is no markup or extra charge to you.

These commissions are paid by the retailer, not by you. It is simply a way for retailers to thank us for connecting you with their products.

Our Affiliate Partners

We participate in the Amazon Associates Program and may partner with other reputable retailers. All affiliate relationships are disclosed throughout the site wherever affiliate links appear.

Amazon and the Amazon logo are trademarks of Amazon.com, Inc. or its affiliates.

How to Identify Affiliate Links

Affiliate links on our site are clearly labeled with buttons like "Check Price on Amazon" or "View on Amazon." You will also see a micro-disclosure near these links that reads: "As an affiliate, we earn from qualifying purchases."

We want you to always know when a link is an affiliate link so you can make informed decisions about where and how you shop.

Our Promise to You

  • We will never recommend a product we would not want for our own family
  • Commission rates never influence our rankings or recommendations
  • We base all recommendations on real customer reviews from verified buyers
  • We update our content regularly to reflect current ratings and availability
  • We clearly label all affiliate links throughout the site

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how we choose products, please do not hesitate to contact us at hello@assistindependence.com.

You can also visit our Contact page to get in touch. We are always happy to hear from you.
